Intelligent Debriefing Analytics

Every incident leaves behind a trail of information like videos, reports, calls, logs, messages, and actions taken under pressure. But when this information remains scattered, organizations lose the most valuable part of an incident: the learning. Intelligent Debriefing with Analytics (IDA) is a unified incident intelligence platform that helps organizations clearly understand what happened, why it happened, and how future incidents can be handled better, all from a single and secure system.

What is IDA?

IDA is an AI-enabled post-incident debriefing and analytics platform that brings together all incident-related data into one structured, searchable, and analyzable view. It helps organizations:

Consolidate incident data from multiple sources
Analyze actions, decisions, and outcomes
Identify gaps, deviations, and root causes
Forecast potential future risks
Improve preparedness, SOPs, and coordination

Key Features

IDA automatically gathers and organizes data from:

  • CCTV and video feeds
  • Audio recordings and call logs
  • GPS, RFID, IoT, and sensor inputs
  • ERP and operational systems
  • Social media content related to incidents

All information is consolidated into a single incident record, eliminating silos.

Instead of manual reviews, IDA presents:

  • Visual timelines of events
  • Interactive dashboards and charts
  • Easy-to-understand summaries

This allows teams to quickly see how the incident unfolded and where decisions mattered most.

IDA structures every review around three core questions:

  • What exactly happened?
  • Why did it happen?
  • How was it handled?

This creates objective, consistent, and actionable debriefs—free from assumptions.

IDA compares actual actions taken during incidents with defined Standard Operating Procedures:

  • Highlights deviations and delays
  • Identifies SOP gaps or outdated steps
  • Supports continuous SOP improvement

Over time, this leads to more realistic procedures and better-trained teams.

By learning from past incidents, IDA:

  • Identifies recurring issues and hidden patterns
  • Detects incidents that may have gone unnoticed earlier
  • Forecasts potential future risks and vulnerabilities

This shifts organizations from reactive response to proactive preparedness.

IDA makes incident information easy to find:

  • Keyword-based search across reports, audio, video, and images
  • Social media hashtag indexing
  • Voice-assisted and NLP-powered search

Critical information is available in seconds, not hours.

  • Automated post-incident report generation
  • Encrypted storage of all incident records
  • Audit-ready documentation for compliance and governance

This reduces administrative effort while ensuring data integrity.

  • Accessible on mobile and tablet devices
  • Supports regional languages and English
  • Designed for both command centers and field teams

Improves coordination during live operations and post-incident reviews.

Key Outcomes

Organizations using IDA benefit from:

Faster and clearer post-incident reviews

Better decision-making during live and future incidents

Stronger SOPs and training programs

Better preparedness for future incidents

Improved coordination across teams

Reduced repetition of the same incidents
