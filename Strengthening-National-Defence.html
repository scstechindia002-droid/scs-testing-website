The business challenge

Operating in one of the world's most densely populated regions, the Indian Air Force required a resilient infrastructure to support:

01
Disaster Recovery and High Availability
Ensuring operations remain online even in the event of system failure or disaster scenarios.
02
Fragmented Incident Handling
Manual workflows, delayed reporting, and siloed systems slowed response to critical incidents.
03
Lack of Unified Visibility
No centralized platform to oversee infrastructure, fleets, geospatial assets, and SOP adherence in real time.
04
Limited Predictive Insight
Inability to identify patterns or forecast threats through incident history or live data streams.

Our Solutions

We implemented a sophisticated demand/load forecasting system designed to minimize utility risks by predicting future consumption requirements with high precision for the largest Power Network in one of India's top metropolitan cities. Our approach leveraged advanced AI/ML techniques.

We modernized distribution systems with cutting-edge SCADA, DMS, IT/OT, ERP, GIS-enabled applications, and ADMS. This project delivered precise forecasting and strategic energy management, with insights through daily, weekly, and monthly reports, DSM charges analysis, and energy portfolio optimization.
