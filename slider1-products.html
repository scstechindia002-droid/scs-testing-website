Intelligent Debriefing With Analytics

Intelligent Debriefing with Analytics (IDA) is a next-generation post-incident debriefing and analytics platform designed to streamline incident reporting, enhance decision-making, and improve operational readiness.

"Turning post-incident chaos into AI-driven intelligence for smarter, faster, and proactive decision-making"

IDA captures, tracks, and consolidates data from past incidents from multiple sources, transforming raw information into a single, AI-powered debriefing report. With AI/ML-driven analytics, the platform not only documents what happened, why it happened, and how it unfolded but also identifies patterns, root causes, and potential future risks. It integrates predictive forecasting to foresee potential future incidents and their underlying causes.

Key Capabilities

Detect Hidden Threats

Integrates multi-source data to identify unnoticed incidents and enable proactive security.

AI-Powered Incident Analysis

Centralizes data from IoT, surveillance, and social channels for a 360° debriefing.

Smarter Decision-Making

Provides AI-driven insights, predictive diagnostics, and root cause analysis.

Automated Resource Optimization

Ensures timely deployment of personnel and benchmarks performance for efficiency.

Seamless Collaboration

Enables real-time communication among officials, emergency services, and stakeholders.

Data-Driven Strategy & Training

Leverages analytics and GIS insights for optimized planning and automated training.

Smart Landslide Early Warning System

Landslides pose a significant threat to communities, infrastructure, and economies. Our Landslide Forecasting Solution is designed to forecast, manage, and mitigate the devastating impact of landslides. Leveraging the power of GIS, AI/ML, and predictive analytics, it equips emergency teams with real-time, accurate intelligence for advanced landslide forecasting and proactive response strategies.

“Predicting risks, safeguarding lives, & enabling proactive disaster resilience”

By integrating data from weather stations, satellite imagery, short-range algorithms, susceptibility maps, climatic spatial layers, exposure vulnerability maps, landslide inventories, density maps, and other static boundary maps, the system delivers highly informed and logical forecasts. These insights are then displayed on a GIS-based dashboard and mobile app, enabling seamless observation, in-depth analytics, and timely action.

Key Capabilities

Early & Accurate Forecasting

AI/ML-driven risk predictions for timely alerts and better preparedness.

Data-Driven Decision Making

Multi-source integration ensures precise analysis and informed response.

Real-Time Monitoring & Alerts

GIS-based dashboards and mobile apps provide live updates and actionable insights.

Enhanced Community Resilience

Helps mitigate risks, minimize economic losses, and save lives.

Improved Emergency Coordination

Enables seamless collaboration between disaster management authorities, rescue teams, and local governments.

Adaptive & Scalable

Customizable for different terrains, ensuring effective risk assessment across diverse landscapes.
