Homeland security and defense

Securing nations. Empowering missions — with intelligence, speed, and control.

SCS Tech builds secure, scalable systems for defense and homeland security agencies using AI, GIS, IoT, cloud, and surveillance analytics, helping teams detect threats faster, coordinate response, and protect critical infrastructure.

How SCS Tech India can help you

Real-time situational awareness across regions and operations

Solutions we Deliver

Real-Time Threat Monitoring & Alerts

AI-powered dashboards that track incidents, anomalies, and risk zones across borders, cities, and critical facilities—live and in one view.

Integrated Command & Control Centers

Centralized command hubs that combine surveillance feeds, incident alerts, unit status, and response coordination for faster decision-making.

Cybersecurity for Defense Systems

Round-the-clock protection against cyber threats with threat analytics, SOC enablement, secure networks, and rapid incident response.

GIS-Based Situational Awareness Platforms

Map threats, assets, routes, and operational zones using location intelligence—supporting quick planning and real-time response.

Surveillance Intelligence & Video Analytics

Enhance monitoring using AI-based detection for cameras, drones, and perimeter systems—turning raw feeds into actionable intelligence.

Emergency & Incident Management Systems

Digitize incident reporting, dispatch workflows, escalation paths, and response tracking—ensuring structured action during critical events.
