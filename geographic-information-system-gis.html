Geographic Information System

With over 15 years of proven expertise, SCS Tech India Pvt Ltd is a trusted GIS companies in India. We transform geography into strategy—empowering organizations to see more, understand faster, and plan smarter. From disaster management and infrastructure planning to environmental monitoring and resource optimization, our intelligent GIS solutions bring clarity to complex challenges. Our offerings go beyond traditional mapping. We deliver high-precision, real-time geospatial systems that accelerate go-to-market, boost operational efficiency, and unlock new revenue opportunities. Backed by cutting-edge technology and deep domain knowledge, SCS Tech powers your platforms with accurate, scalable, and actionable location intelligence—built for impact.

Some Of Our Key Products

Re-engineering Urban Sustainability with Solid Waste Management Solution

We reengineered solid waste management for a Tier-1 city, implementing a GPS and GIS-enabled tracking system. Our solution automates waste collection, optimizes routes, integrates weighbridges, and enhances fleet management, driving efficiency, transparency, and sustainability.

Enhancing Methods To Predict Natural Disasters With An Advanced Landslide Warning System

We deployed a GIS and AI-powered Landslide Warning System across India’s most vulnerable regions—integrating spatial intelligence, real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and a 24/7 forecasting center. Enabled by advanced ICT, the system delivers rapid alerts with high scalability and reliability, enhancing disaster readiness and accelerating response.
