Transforming Urban Mobility with a Smart Metro Rail System

As cities grow denser and more complex, the pressure on public transportation systems to be faster, smarter, and more connected has never been greater. To enable a next-generation metro rail system that supports real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, and automated, fail-safe operations—while enhancing security, efficiency, and passenger satisfaction across the entire network.

The business challenge

01
Lack of Unified Operational Visibility
Disparate systems for surveillance, communication, and access control operated in isolation—lacking unified oversight.
02
Limited Incident Detection & Response
Absence of intelligent video analytics led to manual detection and slow coordination during emergencies.
03
Manual Asset Monitoring
Critical infrastructure lacked real-time diagnostics, limiting predictive maintenance and uptime assurance.
04
Inadequate Access Control
Sensitive zones were not equipped with automated access or intrusion detection, posing security risks.
05
Disconnected Communication Systems
Fragmented voice networks slowed down response and inter-station coordination.
06
No Scalable Digital Backbone
Lack of a robust IT network limited future integration, automation, and expansion.

Our Solutions

SCS Tech implemented a robust IT and Communication Backbone—powering the Smart Metro Rail with real-time visibility, intelligent automation, and secure, uninterrupted connectivity across all stations and transit units.

Centralized Command & Control Center

Integrated control room for unified monitoring of all metro operations, security systems, and communication lines across the network.

AI-Enabled Surveillance Ecosystem

Installation of 3,100+ high-definition CCTV cameras with advanced video analytics to enhance passenger safety, monitor crowd movement, and detect anomalies in real time.

Autonomous Drone Surveillance

Installation of 3,100+ high-definition CCTV cameras with advanced video analytics to enhance passenger safety, monitor crowd movement, and detect anomalies in real time.

Intelligent Communication Backbone

Implementation of a scalable IPBX telephony system ensuring uninterrupted communication between stations, operations, and emergency units.

Smart Access & Intrusion Detection

Comprehensive access control and intrusion detection systems deployed across sensitive zones with real-time alerting and response integration.

High-Speed Fiber Optic Network Infrastructure

Robust FOTS-based networking architecture delivering secure, high-speed transmission of voice, video, and data with zero downtime.

Impact

  • Operational Efficiency: Centralized data, unified communications, and automated workflows enable faster decisions and smoother operations.
  • Enhanced Passenger Experience: Better station flow, security, and response times improve comfort and reliability for daily commuters.
  • Uptime & Reliability: Predictive maintenance reduces outages and ensures uninterrupted service delivery.
  • Future-Ready Infrastructure: Modular systems and scalable architecture prepare the metro network for future tech integration and expansion.
