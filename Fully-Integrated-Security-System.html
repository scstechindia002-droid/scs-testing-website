Revolutionizing Infrastructure Security with a Fully Integrated Security System (FISS)

To secure the full perimeter and internal assets of a large-scale power plant, a Fully Integrated Security System (FISS) was envisioned—an end-to-end command architecture combining AI-driven incident management that enables real-time monitoring, automated incident response, and centralized decision-making—making day-to-day physical security operations smarter, faster, and more resilient.

The business challenge

The plant required a comprehensive, modern security framework that could overcome:

01
Fragmented Systems
Multiple security elements operating in silos, limiting real-time decision-making.
02
Delayed Incident Handling
No centralized monitoring to assess, escalate, and respond to events in real time.
03
Unauthorized Entry Risks
Need to strictly regulate both human and vehicular movement across sensitive zones.
04
Zero Visibility
No integrated dashboard for monitoring camera feeds, perimeter breaches, or dispatch readiness.

Our Solutions

To address the complex security, surveillance, and response challenges of high-value infrastructure, SCS Tech engineered and deployed a Fully Integrated Security System (FISS)——converging 14+ subsystems into one real-time, intelligence-driven ecosystem.

Unified Security Command & Control Center

A centralized platform offering real-time visualization of surveillance zones, alerts, asset movement, and incident workflows across the entire facility.

AI-Driven Incident Management

Automated incident detection, classification, and escalation integrated with voice communication and SOP-based response protocols.

Smart Access & Entry Monitoring

Smart card-enabled access control, integrated with boom barriers and vehicle screening systems to restrict unauthorized human and vehicular movement.

Integrated Voice & Radio Communication

IP-based EPABX and mobile radio systems linked to the command center for uninterrupted field-to-control room communication.

Public Alert & Messaging Systems

Zone-wise IP-based public address systems and message boards for emergency alerts, real-time notifications, and lockdown announcements.

Post-Incident Debriefing & Analysis

Screen recording, voice logs, and event playback tools to facilitate forensic analysis and continuous SOP improvement.

Key Scope of Work Done

Command & GIS-Based Monitoring

Deployed Command & Control App (CCA) with GIS visualization and real-time alerts.

Smart Surveillance & Perimeter Security

Installed HD and thermal cameras with watchtower integration and automated response.

Access & Movement Control

Implemented Truck Tracking System (TTS), Access Control System (ACS) and scanning systems for secure vehicle and personnel entry.

High-Availability Data & DR Setup

Central SAN/NAS data center with disaster recovery for uninterrupted operations.

Live Dashboards & Incident Debriefing

70” video walls with advanced alarm/event analysis tools.

Secure Connectivity & O&M Support

MPLS/P2P networking, cross-device access, training, and full facility management.

Impact

  • Real-Time Visibility Across the Fleet: Live plant performance metrics accessible to HQ for operational benchmarking and cross-station coordination.
  • Predictive Decision-Making: AI/ML-driven early warnings reduce unplanned downtime, improve asset reliability, and optimize maintenance windows.
  • Improved Generation Efficiency: Set-point and soot blowing optimization reduce fuel costs, enhance thermal performance, and support DSM alignment.
  • Scalable, Future-Ready Infrastructure : Built to support future modules, including AI forecasting, renewable integration, and centralized dispatch control.
