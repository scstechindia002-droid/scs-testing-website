Enhancing the efficiency of Energy Distribution and Network infrastructure with Enterprise GIS and Network Analysis

To deliver uninterrupted energy with precision and foresight, an Enterprise GIS and Network Analysis Solution was implemented—enabling one of India’s largest power distribution companies to make faster, smarter, and data-driven infrastructure decisions.

The business challenge

The client was facing several critical challenges that impacted grid reliability, operational efficiency, and infrastructure planning:

01
Disjointed Systems
Manual processes and isolated legacy applications led to planning delays, data duplication, and visibility gaps.
02
Inefficient Load Distribution
Lack of spatial intelligence resulted in sub-optimal feeder design, increased losses, and delayed fault detection.
03
Limited Analytical Capability
The absence of integrated geospatial analysis hindered proactive decision-making.
04
Data Discrepancies
Variability and inconsistency in the GIS database created operational blind spots.
05
Scalability Gaps
Existing systems could not support future expansions or integration with advanced tools like SCADA or Load Forecasting.

Our Solutions

To overcome these roadblocks, SCS Tech implemented a state-wide Enterprise GIS and Network Analysis solution—digitizing the client’s entire power distribution ecosystem and turning spatial data into a real-time decision-making engine.

Centralized Geodatabase

Unified all legacy GIS datasets into a standard, state-wide model with real-time data cleaning, migration, and validation

System-Wide Integration

Seamless integration of GIS with 20+ operational modules including SCADA, Load Forecasting, MDAS, SAP-ERP, and mobile applications.

Interactive GIS Dashboards

Enhancing grid stability through responsive load management.

Advanced Network Analysis Tools

Power flow simulation, feeder reconfiguration, outage analysis, and load balancing—optimized through integrated NA tools.

Cloud-Based Deployment

Secure, scalable deployment of GIS and NA platforms on the cloud, ensuring 24x7 availability and minimal downtime.

Training & Facility Management

Ongoing state-wide training programs and full-time facility management services to support users, maintain systems, and drive adoption.

Key Scope of Work Done

Centralized GIS Database Setup

Preparation and migration of a unified GIS geodatabase to streamline spatial data management.

Deployment of GIS & Network Analysis Solutions

Implementation of Enterprise GIS and advanced Network Analysis tools for intelligent grid planning.

Seamless System Integration

Integration with 20+ core utility systems including SCADA, ERP, MDAS, and Load Forecasting for operational synergy.

Real-Time Monitoring Dashboards

Development of dynamic GIS-based dashboards for live grid monitoring and analytical insights.

Cloud-Based High Availability Infrastructure

Deployment on a secure, scalable cloud environment ensuring continuous access and system reliability.

Licensing, Training & Ongoing Support

Provision of COTS software, user interfaces, and state-wide technical support with training and facility management services.

Impact

  • Improved Load Distribution: Optimized network design and real-time analytics significantly reduced energy losses and improved service reliability.
  • Faster, Smarter Decisions: Interactive dashboards enabled leadership to act on real-time insights rather than outdated reports or static spreadsheets.
  • End-to-End Visibility: Geospatial intelligence provided a single-pane-of-glass view across the state—connecting assets, feeders, substations, and loads in one system.
  • Seamless System Interoperability: Deep integration with mission-critical platforms ensured smooth workflows and end-to-end process automation.
